Program Highlights
课程特色

A specific focus on succession issues of ethnic Chinese family businesses: unfolding secrets of how they retain control beyond three generations and how they differ from other ethnic groups

重点关注华人家族企业的传承问题：揭示家族如何富过三代，永续企业的控制权，并探讨华人和其他族群的家族企业之分别

A case-based program led by our faculty with years of practical experience and academic excellence, and guest speakers from leading family businesses such as Lee Kum Kee, Orient Overseas Container Line (OOCL) and Esquel Group

课程以案例为基础，由具多年实战经验和卓越学术研究成果的家族企业教授团队领导，特邀嘉宾来自如李锦记、东方海外和溢达集团等卓越的家族企业

Introduce the roles of family governance, family offices, trusts and foundations in preserving family wealth, harmony and legacy

介绍家族治理、家族办公室、家族信托和家族基金会在保持家族财富、和谐以及价值观的作用

A specialized lecture in arts and wine investment, with a guided tour in an auction preview and a wine-pairing dinner tailored by a wine specialist from a Jewish family business

参加艺术品和葡萄酒投资讲座，拍卖会预展专人导览，和犹太家族企业葡萄酒专家主持的葡萄酒配对晚宴

Pay a special visit to the private residence of a prominent shipping family business; listen to their third generation leader sharing their family history, legacy and challenges

造访一个香港显赫的航运家族企业的私人住所，倾听他们第三代领导人分享家族历史、传统和挑战
